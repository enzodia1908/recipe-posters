Harissa Prawn + Bacon Orzo
Meal Prep · 4 Serves · Follow Stage by Stage
4
serves
~50 min
total time
180°c
oven temp
1 pan
to wash
!
Remember: Do all your prep in Stage 2 before anything hits the pan — it makes the whole cook calm and straightforward.
1
Setup
before you start
  • Preheat oven 180°c · 160°c fan
  • Get 1 frying pan + 1 baking dish
2
Prep everything
do all before cooking
  • Chop onion 150g · finely
  • Slice 2 cloves garlic
  • Measure risoni 240g
  • Measure vegetable stock 600ml
  • Measure harissa 1.5 tbsp
  • Chop dill ~10g
  • Cut lemon in half
  • Pat dry prawns 600g
  • Dice bacon 120–150g
3
Cook the bacon
stovetop · 3–5 min
  • Add bacon to pan no oil needed
  • Cook until lightly crispy 3–5 mins · medium heat
  • Remove bacon · set aside — leave fat in pan
4
Cook the base
stovetop · ~15 min
  • Add onion · cook until soft 6–8 mins
  • Add garlic · cook 30 sec
  • Add tomatoes (300g) + 2 tbsp water cook 5 mins
5
Add orzo + transfer to oven
180°c · 10–12 min
  • Add risoni (240g), stock (600ml), 1 tbsp harissa, half the dill + bacon
  • Bring to simmer
  • Pour into baking dish · spread evenly · bake
6
Prep prawns
while oven bakes
  • Toss prawns with ½ tbsp harissa + juice of ½ lemon
  • Add smoked paprika ½–1 tsp
7
Add spinach + prawns
180°c · 8–10 min
  • Remove dish · stir in spinach (120g)
  • Add prawns on top · push slightly into orzo
  • Bake until prawns are pink and cooked through
8
Finish + pack
done!
  • Top with remaining dill + lemon wedges
  • Split into 4 containers · close lids · kitchen done
